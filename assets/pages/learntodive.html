As a branch of the British Sub Aqua Club (BSAC), we run courses for the various BSAC qualifications, starting with the entry-level Ocean Diver grade. Within a year of joining the club it's possible to become a competent diver at BSAC's Sports Diver grade. Free instruction is one of the many benefits of joining SASAC. For details about the Ocean Diver and Sports Diver courses click here. For a video introduction to the club click here.

Why not come down to the club for a try dive before you decide if you want to join the Ocean Diver Course. Try dives take place on the first Wednesday of each month; after an introductory briefing at the clubhouse you'll be taken on a dive at Westminster Lodge Leisure Centre under the careful supervision of a club instructor. All equipment is provided and the try dive will cost £10. For details call: 01727 859 829 or submit the contact request form below.

Scuba Diving Courses

Ocean Diver

What
Entry level qualification
Where
SASAC clubhouse; Westmister Lodge Leisure Centre; Stoney Cove dive centre
When
Eight Wednesday evenings and two Sundays. Can be completed within three months.
Description
Seven theory lessons at clubhouse followed by a written exam. Five of the theory lectures are also followed by pool lessons at Westminster Lodge Leisure Centre. Course also includes five open-water dives carried out over two days at the Stoney Cove dive centre in Leicestershire

Sports Diver

What
Qualifies you to dive with others of the same grade
Where
SASAC clubhouse; Westmister Lodge Leisure Centre; Stoney Cove dive centre; Swanage, Dorset
When
Seven Wednesday evenings and two Sundays. Can be completed within six months
Description
Six theory lessons at clubhouse followed by a written exam. One of the theory lectures is followed by a pool lesson at Westminster Lodge Leisure Centre. Course also includes five open-water dives carried out over two days at Stoney Cove dive centre in Leicestershire and one day at Swanage in Dorset
